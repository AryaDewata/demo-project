SAMURAI WORLD

Samurai world nfts themed in the Edo era, there is often inter-clan warfare (PVP), we need to strengthen our character, in mini-games, we can do, Gathering, Mining, crafting, raiding (PVE), every activity has every nft that can be traded. You as a samurai can have the opportunity to become a landlord by opening bundle Packs, you can explore mining caves using your tools and collect valuable resources, Your character can farm in farmland, you can fight in raid dungeon their chance to drop some special equipment, if you want to be crafter you can work at blacksmith your character automatically repair and craft every request from another player.
